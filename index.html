Bugatti Chiron Super

Equipped with an 8.0-litre 16-cylinder engine rated at 1,577 horsepower, it can reach a top speed of 489 km/h. In fact, Bugatti set a record by becoming the first automaker to top 300 miles per hour.

.Achieving a speed of exactly 304.773 mph (490.484 km/h), it was the fastest production hyper sports car ever. The Chiron Super Sport 300+ bodywork has been extended and aerodynamically optimized for extremely high-speed performance.
